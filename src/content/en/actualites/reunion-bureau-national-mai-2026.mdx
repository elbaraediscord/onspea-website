The National Executive Bureau of ONSPEA held its periodic meeting on **May 15, 2026**, at the national headquarters in Algiers, under the chairmanship of the National President.

## Agenda

Bureau members examined the following points:

- Update on the national economic situation and its impact on member companies
- Follow-up on ongoing files with relevant ministries
- Preparation for the next National Economic Forum
- Miscellaneous questions

## Adopted Resolutions

Following the deliberations, the Bureau adopted several resolutions aimed at strengthening the representation of Algerian entrepreneurs with public institutions.

*ONSPEA reaffirms its commitment to constructive dialogue between economic actors and public authorities.*
